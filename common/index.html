Transforming Kitchen Health
through Cutting-Edge Innovation

Our journey began with a deep dive into the hidden dangers lurking within conventional wood-based kitchen materials. Recognising the urgent need for a safer, healthier alternative, we embarked on a mission to develop a groundbreaking solution. Through years of meticulous research and development, we introduced Silverstone, a revolutionary 100% safe stone infused with the potent antibacterial properties of pure silver.

Every aspect of our Wellness Kitchens, from the sleek cabinets to the sturdy countertops and elegant backsplashes, is meticulously crafted using Silverstone™. This dedication to quality and safety has not only redefined the standard for kitchen materials but has also earned us the prestigious title of the "World's Safest & Strongest Kitchen" not once, but twice.

Join us in our commitment to creating kitchen environments that prioritize the well-being of families worldwide. Together, let's embark on a journey towards safer, healthier living for one and for all.”

Features

Highly UV Resistant

Porcelain slabs maintain their colors even when exposed to continuous, direct sunlight or in contact with atmospheric a gents or smog.

Frost Resistant

The Denser constructions of porcelain tiles makes them frost resistant, as when temperatures drop, these tiles do not get frost damage.

Waterproof

Porcelain Slabs are waterproof and non-porous and the water does not damage the surface or the body of the slab.

Fire Resistance

These are not flammable, reduce the spread of flames, do not burn, and do not emit toxic fumes.

Resistant to stain

Porcelain tiles are waterproof, liquid can’t damage the tile

Resistant to Scratches

Porcelain slabs have a high resistance to scratches.

Recyclable

Porcelain slabs maintain their colors even when exposed to continuous, direct sunlight or in contact with atmospheric a gents or smog.

Resistant to Chemicals

Porcelain slabs do not stain and resist the most aggressive chemical agents..

Easy to Clean

Resistant of High Temperatures

A porcelain slab floor will not change as a result of sharp temperature fluctuations.
